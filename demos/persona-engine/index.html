What It Does

Three core operations that power an infinite mythic ecosystem.

Generate

Create unique AI personas from archetypal foundations. Each entity inherits traits, domains, and elemental affinities from the six genesis archetypes.

Synthesize

Combine personas to spawn new hybrid entities with inherited traits. Two parent archetypes merge, producing offspring with blended powers and new domains.

Evolve

Track lineage, analyze evolution patterns, and watch your pantheon grow. Every persona knows its ancestors and can spawn new generations.
